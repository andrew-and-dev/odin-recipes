Caesar Salad

Home page.

Description

A classic Caesar salad with crisp romaine lettuce, homemade creamy Caesar dressing, crunchy croutons, and freshly grated Parmesan cheese. This iconic salad is perfect as a side dish or add grilled chicken for a complete meal!

Designed by Small Caesar Salad at Kapp's Pizza Bar & Grill

Ingredients

  • 1 large head romaine lettuce, chopped
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 cups croutons
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 anchovy fillets (optional)
  • 1 large egg yolk
  • 2 tablespoons fresh lemon juice
  • 1 teaspoon Dijon mustard
  • 1 teaspoon Worcestershire sauce
  • 1/2 cup extra virgin olive oil
  • Salt and black pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Wash and thoroughly dry the romaine lettuce. Chop into bite-sized pieces and refrigerate until ready to serve.
  2. In a small bowl, mash the garlic and anchovies (if using) into a paste using a fork.
  3. Add the egg yolk, lemon juice, Dijon mustard, and Worcestershire sauce to the garlic paste. Whisk together until well combined.
  4. While whisking constantly, slowly drizzle in the olive oil to create a creamy emulsion. The dressing should be thick and smooth.
  5. Season the dressing with salt and pepper to taste. Add more lemon juice if needed.
  6. In a large bowl, toss the chopped romaine lettuce with the Caesar dressing until evenly coated.
  7. Add the croutons and half of the Parmesan cheese. Toss gently to combine.
  8. Transfer to serving plates and top with the remaining Parmesan cheese.
  9. Serve immediately while the lettuce is crisp and cold.
